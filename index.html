TJ Dawe

Actor, Writer, Director

'...if George Carlin and Spalding Grey had a baby...'

TJ Dawe is an award winning (and published) writer/performer/director. Current projects: Burn Job, PostSecret: the Show, and One Woman Sex and the City: a Parody on Love, Frienship and Shoes. Various credits include Medicine,Marathon, The Slipknot, The Power of Ignorance, Lucky 9, 52 Pick-up and Dishpig. He did a TEDx talk. He co-wrote the play Toothpaste and Cigars, which has been made into the movie The F Word, starring Daniel Radcliffe.

'Dawe is a skilled performer, playing with tone and pace, punctuating his physical stillness with sudden bouts of manic movement. Here, more than ever, he’s an honest performer. More than once, his openheartedness brought tears to my eyes.' -Georgia Straight

Kahlil Ashanti

Actor, Writer, Business Nerd

'...if Bernie Mac and Eddie Murphy had a baby...'

Kahlil began performing as a stand-up at age 14 in comedy clubs, later joining the US Air Force and touring as a performer. He's performed magic at Caesars Palace, was selected twice for Cirque du Soleil and he took his solo show from the Fringe to a successful Off-Broadway run and two sellout seasons (and a Fringe First award) at the Edinburgh Fringe.

'Amid the hype fuelled blast of any arts festival comes at least a dozen wheezing promises that here, at last, is The Next Big Thing. In the case of the unlawfully gifted Kahlil Ashanti, the promise happens, on this occasion, to be completely, marvellously true. The man is a star.'
-Melbourne Age
